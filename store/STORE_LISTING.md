## Full description (paste into Play Console)
MedCompanion AI helps you understand your health in plain, human language — so you walk into your next doctor’s appointment feeling informed, not lost.

It is NOT a doctor, NOT a diagnosis, and NOT a replacement for medical care. Think of it as the knowledgeable friend you call when something feels off: it listens, helps you make sense of what you’re experiencing, and prepares you to have a better conversation with your real care team.

WHY PEOPLE USE IT
• Describe how you feel in your own words — any language, by typing or by voice.
• Get a warm, easy-to-understand briefing about what it might relate to.
• Walk in with the right questions to ask your doctor.
• Check medications, foods, and drinks for interactions to watch for.

BUILT TO WORK WITH YOUR DOCTOR — NOT INSTEAD OF THEM
Unlike “Dr. Google,” MedCompanion AI is designed to support your relationship with your clinician. It never tells you to argue with your doctor, change your medication on your own, or treat its information as a diagnosis. Your care team knows your full history and always has the final say.

CHOOSE WHO IT’S FOR
• For myself — understand what I’m feeling.
• For a loved one — know how to help and what to ask.
• For medical professionals — clinical, evidence-based briefings.
• Medication & interaction check — what’s safe to mix.

PRIVATE & RESPECTFUL
We don’t sell your information and we don’t use your health questions for advertising. Read our policy: https://medcompanion-ai.up.railway.app/privacy

IMPORTANT — PLEASE READ
MedCompanion AI provides general health information for educational purposes only. It is not a medical device, not a diagnosis, and not a substitute for professional medical advice. It does not handle emergencies. If you may be experiencing a medical emergency, call your local emergency number (911 in the US) right away.
