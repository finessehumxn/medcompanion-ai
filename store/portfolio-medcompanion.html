Case Study · Healthcare AI

MedCompanion AI

A safety-first AI health companion that helps everyday people understand their health in plain language — and partners with their doctor instead of replacing them. Spanning the entire visit: before, during, and after.

View the live product → Source on GitHub

The problem

People leave doctor visits confused, and tools like WebMD or ChatGPT often make it worse — sending patients in to argue with their clinician. MedCompanion was built to do the opposite: reduce fear, build understanding, and make the patient a prepared partner in their own care.

What it does

Doctor Bridge

Turns every briefing into a printable, shareable Doctor Visit Sheet — questions to ask, plain-language summary, and sources — so patients hand over a brief instead of an argument.

Live Visit Companion

A real-time, in-the-exam-room mode that decodes what the doctor just said and suggests the next smart question — then compiles an after-visit summary.

Reviewed by a Real Doctor

A physician-review portal lets licensed clinicians co-sign briefings, surfacing a "verified by a physician" badge.

Multilingual & Read-Aloud

Reads results aloud and translates them into the patient's language while the clinician keeps an English handoff — built for accessibility and equity.

Safety-first pipeline

A LangGraph state machine with an explicit human-in-the-loop step, crisis/emergency guardrails, an ER-or-not triage, and red-flag detection.

Health Memory

A private health log that turns logged symptoms into a dual patient + clinical visit-prep, plus a new-patient one-pager.
