Medical Chronology Builder

Turn a client's medical records into a factual, dated timeline — a draft in seconds, then verify against source. Built for paralegals and attorneys on injury & med-mal matters.

Factual organization only — this tool does not give legal or medical advice, and does not opine on causation, fault, or damages. Always verify every entry against the source records.
Nothing is stored on our servers — the records are organized and returned, not retained.
